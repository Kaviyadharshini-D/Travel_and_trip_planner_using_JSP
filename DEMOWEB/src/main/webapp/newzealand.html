New Zealand Adventure Itinerary

Day 1: Auckland

  • Explore the Auckland Sky Tower and enjoy panoramic views.
  • Visit the Auckland War Memorial Museum.
  • Take a stroll in the Auckland Domain and Wintergardens.
  • Explore the vibrant waterfront area and Viaduct Harbour.

Day 2: Rotorua

  • Drive to Rotorua, known for its geothermal activity.
  • Visit Te Puia to see geysers and Maori cultural performances.
  • Relax at the Polynesian Spa and experience the hot springs.

Day 3: Taupo

  • Drive to Taupo and enjoy the scenic views of Lake Taupo.
  • Visit Huka Falls and take a boat cruise on the lake.
  • Optional: Skydiving or bungee jumping for adventure enthusiasts.

Day 4: Queenstown

  • Fly to Queenstown, the adventure capital of New Zealand.
  • Explore the town, visit Queenstown Gardens, and take the Skyline Gondola.
  • Day trip to Milford Sound for a scenic cruise or visit nearby Fiordland National Park.

Day 5: Wanaka

  • Drive to Wanaka, known for its stunning lake and mountain scenery.
  • Visit Puzzling World and enjoy water activities on Lake Wanaka.
  • Hike or bike the trails around the area.
