Goa Adventure Itinerary

Day 1: Beach Day

  • Relax on the sandy beaches of North Goa.
  • Explore popular beaches like Calangute and Baga.
  • Enjoy water sports activities like parasailing or jet-skiing.
  • Experience the vibrant beach shacks and nightlife.

Day 2: Cultural Exploration

  • Visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus in Old Goa.
  • Explore the Se Cathedral and St. Augustine Tower.
  • Discover the rich history at the Archaeological Museum.
  • Stroll through the charming streets of Fontainhas.

Day 3: Adventure in Dudhsagar

  • Embark on a thrilling jeep safari to Dudhsagar Waterfalls.
  • Experience the breathtaking views of the cascading falls.
  • Enjoy a refreshing swim in the natural pool.
  • Explore the surrounding lush forests and wildlife.

Day 4: Spice Plantations and River Cruise

  • Visit a spice plantation for a guided tour.
  • Learn about various spices and their cultivation.
  • Enjoy a traditional Goan lunch at the plantation.
  • Take a scenic river cruise in the evening.

Day 5: Panaji and Shopping

  • Explore the capital city, Panaji.
  • Visit the historic Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception.
  • Shop for souvenirs at the colorful markets.
  • Relax by the Mandovi River and enjoy local cuisine.
