London Adventure Itinerary

Day 1: Explore Central London

  • Visit the iconic Big Ben and Houses of Parliament.
  • Stroll along the River Thames and enjoy views of the London Eye.
  • Explore the historic Westminster Abbey.
  • Visit Buckingham Palace and witness the Changing of the Guard.

Day 2: British Museum and Covent Garden

  • Explore the British Museum and discover its vast collection.
  • Stroll through Covent Garden for shopping and street performances.
  • Visit the Royal Opera House.
  • Enjoy a meal at one of the many restaurants in the area.

Day 3: Tower of London and Tower Bridge

  • Explore the historic Tower of London and see the Crown Jewels.
  • Walk across Tower Bridge and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
  • Visit the nearby Shard for breathtaking views from the observation deck.

Day 4: Parks and Museums

  • Relax in Hyde Park or Kensington Gardens.
  • Visit the Natural History Museum or the Victoria and Albert Museum.
  • Explore the trendy neighborhoods of Notting Hill or Camden Town.

Day 5: Day Trip to Windsor Castle

  • Take a day trip to Windsor Castle, the oldest and largest occupied castle in the world.
  • Explore the State Apartments and St. George's Chapel.
  • Enjoy a walk in the beautiful Windsor Great Park.
