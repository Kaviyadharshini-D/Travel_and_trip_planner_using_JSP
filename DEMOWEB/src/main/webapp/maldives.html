Maldives Escape Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paradise

  • Arrive at Male International Airport and transfer to your resort by speedboat or seaplane.
  • Check-in and enjoy the first day at leisure on the beautiful white-sand beaches.
  • Relax and unwind, taking in the stunning views of the turquoise Indian Ocean.

Day 2: Snorkeling Adventure

  • Explore the vibrant coral reefs with a guided snorkeling excursion.
  • Encounter colorful marine life, including tropical fish and maybe even turtles.
  • Relax on the beach and enjoy the crystal-clear waters.

Day 3: Island Hopping

  • Embark on a day of island hopping to nearby atolls and sandbanks.
  • Experience the unique culture of local Maldivian islands.
  • Enjoy a traditional Maldivian lunch on a deserted island.

Day 4: Sunset Cruise and Dining

  • Take a romantic sunset cruise on a traditional dhoni (Maldivian boat).
  • Indulge in a special beachfront dinner with Maldivian and international cuisine.
  • Stargaze on the beach and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere.

Day 5: Water Villa Experience

  • Upgrade to a water villa for the ultimate Maldivian experience.
  • Relax in your private overwater bungalow and enjoy direct access to the lagoon.
  • Depart from the Maldives with unforgettable memories.
