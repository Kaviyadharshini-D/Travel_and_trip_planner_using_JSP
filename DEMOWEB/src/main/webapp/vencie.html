Venice Discovery Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Venice

  • Arrive in Venice and check into your hotel.
  • Take a leisurely evening stroll through the charming streets.
  • Enjoy a traditional Italian dinner at a local trattoria.
  • Admire the beauty of St. Mark's Square in the evening.

Day 2: Explore St. Mark's Square

  • Visit St. Mark's Basilica and marvel at its stunning architecture.
  • Climb to the top of the Campanile for panoramic views of the city.
  • Take a gondola ride along the Grand Canal.
  • Explore the charming streets and bridges of Venice.

Day 3: Murano and Burano Islands

  • Take a boat trip to Murano to witness glassblowing demonstrations.
  • Visit the colorful island of Burano known for its lace-making tradition.
  • Enjoy a seafood dinner in Burano.

Day 4: Venice's Art and History

  • Explore the Gallerie dell'Accademia for Venetian art.
  • Visit the Doge's Palace to experience Venetian history and architecture.
  • Take a scenic walk along the Rialto Bridge.

Day 5: Departure

  • Spend your final morning in Venice exploring any remaining sights or shopping.
  • Depart from Venice with wonderful memories of your Italian adventure.
